Systems Administrator with aspirations to be a Developer

    


I am currently employed by nexAir, which is a local company in Memphis. nexAir is an industrial gas and welding supply company which was founded in 1940. We have around 70 locations ranging between plants to retail stores to distribution centers. I have been with the company for three years and have enjoyed the investment I have made. My specific role is Systems Administrator and Support Lead for nexAir's Information Technology Department. I am the primary Administrator of our instance of Microsoft Endpoint Manager and I assist in managing various resources in our Azure enviornment. From the Support side, I lead our HelpDesk to assist in troubleshooting issues and escalations. The last few months I have been working on a small team to integrate a data warehouse solution using Azure Synapse. One of my best accomplishments is building a Windows Presentation Foundation application which runs on all of our retail counters. This application works as a utility shell for various retail uses, such as our ERP system. On my personal time, I am working to master Python, SQL, JavaScript, and C# as I feel those are four fundamental languages which will pave the way for years to come. I would love to become a Cloud Developer, but my aspiration is to eventually work with Artificial Intelligence and specifically focus on reducing AI predicability.
