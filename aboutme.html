  As you probably know by now, my name is Chad Thweatt. I am currently 32 years old and work as a Systems Administrator at nexAir in Memphis, TN. I was born and raised in Memphis and went to high school at Southern Baptist Educational Center. I am an avid sports fan, specifically am a huge fan of the Memphis Grizzlies and Philadelphia Eagles. One of my favorite hobbies outside of programming related projects is to play guitar. The acoustic sound is what I prefer, so you can find me playing on my Takamine at least 30 minutes to an hour a day. I have written songs, but I am not brave enought to reveal them to anyone... yet. My typical weekend is spent walking/playing with my two dogs, Bellatrix and Xena, working on various projects (both programming and non-programming related), playing acoustic guitar (it is my therapy) watching/analyzing sports, reading a good book, and spending time with my girlfriend, Breanna. I love playing computer games, but I don't have as much time to play nowadays. When I do have the time, I love to play Destiny 2, Rocket League, & horror games.
