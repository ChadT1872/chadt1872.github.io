I would like to state that I understand the issue surrounding AI art generation and the threat it could pose to real artists. I am of the opinion artists should be compensated when their style is used, somewhat like a royalty for subscription services. To add, I think it should be more so used for inspiration. For example, Moon Thief is an inspiration for a book I want to write, however I will never even think about using the image for profit.
